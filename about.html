About Me

My name is Erica Joshua Manyama, and I am from Tanzania, East Africa. I am currently a third-year student at Lovely Professional University, majoring in Data Science and Data Engineering. I consider myself a passionate and curious individual who enjoys learning and solving problems through data. My interest in data stems from a curiosity about how information and patterns can help explain real-world challenges and support better decision-making.

Throughout my academic journey, I have developed experience working with tools such as Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI to clean, analyze, and visualize data effectively. I enjoy exploring datasets, identifying trends, and transforming complex information into clear insights that help people better understand data.

Alongside my technical skills, I value strong soft skills such as attention to detail, problem solving, teamwork, and clear communication, which enable me to effectively interpret and present data insights.

Outside of academics, I enjoy participating in sports ⚽ and exploring new places 🌍. These experiences allow me to stay active, explore new cultures, and gain new perspectives that inspire both personal and professional growth.

My goal is to build a career where I am considered a valuable asset and can use data to help organizations make informed decisions and create meaningful impact.
